The Best Movies according to Yazhiniyan

My top 3 Tamil Movies of all time

Asuran

Absolute master class of vetri maran describing that cast discrimination still exist in the country.
Peak cinematic adventure moralising that education is only weapon to overcome the struggle

Jai Bhim

T G Gnanavel's legal drama and investigation thriller showcased the police bias, state brutality, and the fight for justice.
Special mention to manikandan who's acting is so realistic.

Meiyazhagan

Feel good movie of life time .Best Cinematic experience .
Watches often.Doesnt bore.
